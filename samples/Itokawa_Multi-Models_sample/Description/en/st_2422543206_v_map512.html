Texture Map (st_2422543206_v_map512)

Global surface texture from AMICA camera imagery — real visual appearance

This is a photographic texture map created by mosaicking AMICA images onto the shape model. Unlike slope or geopotential maps, this shows the actual visual appearance of Itokawa as seen by the camera — color, pattern, and brightness as they look in visible light.
