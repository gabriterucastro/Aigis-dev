Gravitational Slope (itokawa_slope 20110111)

Surface slope angle relative to effective gravity direction — computed 2011-01-11

Gravitational slope is the angle between the surface normal and the effective gravity vector (gravitation + centrifugal force). High slope → steep surface → material cannot remain. Low slope → flat → regolith settles. Itokawa's elongated shape creates a particularly complex gravity field with large slope variations.
