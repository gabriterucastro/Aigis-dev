Geopotential Height (itokawa_Gpotential 20110111)

Where material naturally flows — computed 2011-01-11

Geopotential height is the effective topographic height accounting for both gravity and rotation. Material spontaneously flows from high to low geopotential, like water flowing downhill. On Itokawa (rotation period 12.132 hours), the complex elongated shape creates a highly irregular potential field.
