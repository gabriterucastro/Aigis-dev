Itokawa Elevation (64-facet reference)

Surface relief map — deviation from a simplified 64-facet reference ellipsoid

Elevation here is the deviation of the actual surface from a simplified 64-facet polyhedron reference shape. Unlike geopotential height, this is purely geometric — how much the surface protrudes above or sinks below the reference shape. Boulders appear as high points; MUSES Sea appears as a low depression.
